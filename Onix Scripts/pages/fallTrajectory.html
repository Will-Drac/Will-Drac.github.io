Fall Trajectory

This mod uses information from my Player Physics library to predict where the player will land when falling.

It uses the equation $$ {x_f=x_i+v_{ix}t+\frac{1}{2}a_x t^2} $$ to do this. See Pearl Targeter for more of this fella. Every frame, it predicts the path in the future and checks it for intersections with blocks. If it finds an intersection, that's where the player will land and it displays that as a target on the screen.
